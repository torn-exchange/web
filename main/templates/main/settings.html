Tutorial: Welcome to your settings
  • Here you can make changes to enhance your TE experience - emojis are supported in all of the text boxes if you wish to make your listings more fun!
  • If you are a job seeker, reviver or you are selling losses, just tick the corresponding box and fill in the message box with your prices so that they appear in the appropriate listings.
  • If you wish to stop advertising your revives or losses, simply un-tick the box and you will no longer appear in the search. NOTE: You do not need to erase the custom text.
  • If you are a trader, you can add a custom message to the top of your price list by entering your text in the Price List Description.
  • If you have a forum thread with reviews and ratings, you can link it here and it will appear as a Reviews button on your price list.
  • If you don't want to see tutorial messages any more once you have gotten used to the website, un-tick the box and they will no longer appear at the top of each page. You can always show them again if you need a recap!
